Lesson 16 – Phone & WhatsApp Arabic

Talk like a real brother in WhatsApp groups: voice notes, texting shortcuts, checking up on people, and arranging plans.

Level: A2 → B1 Topic: Casual messaging Platform: WhatsApp Time: ~90 minutes

1. Core Expressions – Phone / Messaging

ArabicTranslitMeaning
وِينك؟wēnak?Where are you?
شَخْبَارَك؟shakhbārak?How you doing? (Najdi)
رَدّ عَلَيَّradd ʿalayyareply to me
أَرْسِل لِي صُورَةarsil lī ṣūrahsend me a picture
صَوْتِي وَاضِح؟ṣōtī wāḍiḥ?is my voice clear?
وَاتْس؟wats?What's up? / WhatsApp?

Short Text Style

  • وينك؟
  • شلونك؟
  • بجيك الحين

2. WhatsApp Chat Flow – Real Example

Short, casual, friendly.

أ: وِينك؟
ب: فِي البَيْت
أ: يَلّا مَقْهَى؟
ب: دَقِيقَة، أَلْبِس وَأَجِي
أ: شَخْبَارَك؟ كُلّ شَيّ تَمَام؟
ب: الحمد لله، وإنت؟
A: Where are you?
B: At home
A: Let’s go to the café?
B: One minute, I’ll get dressed and come
A: Everything good?
B: Al-ḥamdulillah, and you?

3. Checking on People (Very Arabic)

طَمِّنِّي عَنك – ṭamminnī ʿannak – reassure me about you

مُشْتَاق لَك – mushtāg lak – I miss you

كُل شَيّ طَيِّب؟ – kull shayy ṭayyib? – everything okay?

Audio Drill

Record and send these as voice notes to self / friend:

  • طَمِّنِّي عَنك
  • كُل شَيّ طَيِّب؟
  • شنو الأخبار؟

4. Missed Calls & “Call Me Back”

  • مَعْلِيش فَاتِنِي مُكَالَمَتَك – maʿlēsh fātinī mukālamatak – sorry I missed your call
  • أدَقّ عَلَيْك بَعْدِين – adagg ʿalēk baʿdēn – I’ll call you later
  • كَلِّمْنِي لَمَّا تِفْضَى – kallimnī lammā tifḍā – call me when you're free
  • إذَا تَقْدَر دَقّ عَلَيَّ – idhā tegdar dagg ʿalayya – if you can, call me

5. Arab Group Chat Culture

Typical Messages
  • يَجْمَاعَة! – yā jəmāʿah! – guys!
  • مَنْ يَجِي المَسْجِد؟ – man yījī il-masjid? – who’s coming masjid?
  • وَاحِد يَرُوح يَدُقّ قَهْوَة
Send these in a group
  • وينكم؟
  • من يجي؟

6. End-of-Lesson Quiz

  1. Translate: “Where are you?” (m)
  2. Say: “Call me later.”
  3. Say: “Everything good?” (natural Gulf/Najdi)
Sample Answers
  • وينك؟
  • أدقّ عليك بعدين
  • كُل شَيّ طَيِّب؟ / شخبارك؟
