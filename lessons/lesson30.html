Phase 2 Review: Real-life Conversations

Lesson 30 – Review 11–20

Past tense, future plans, telling short stories, inviting people out, complaining politely, WhatsApp Arabic, travel, restaurants, and talking about people’s personality – all in one review.

Level: A2 → early B1 Focus: Past & future + social talk Time: 90–100 minutes

1️⃣ Past Tense – Quick Recap

If you can’t talk about yesterday, you can’t really chat. This is from Lessons 11–12 & 21.

English Arabic Translit
I went رُحْت ruḥt
I ate أَكَلْت akalt
I drank شِرِبْت shiribt
I worked شْتَغَلْت ishtaghalt / shtaghalt
I studied دَرَسْت darast
Mini drill – say it about yesterday
  1. Yesterday I went to the masjid.
  2. Yesterday I worked a lot.
  3. Yesterday I ate with my family.
Sample answers
  • امس رُحْت المَسْجِد.
  • امس شْتَغَلْت كَثِير.
  • امس أَكَلْت مَع أَهْلِي.

2️⃣ Future – Plans & “Gonna”

From Lesson 13 – this is how you sound like “I’m gonna…” in Saudi/Gulf style.

Pattern Arabic Example meaning
raḥ + verb رَاح أَرُوح I’m going to go
b- + verb (Gulf-ish) بَرُوح I’m gonna go
ba- + verb بَأَدْرُس I’m gonna study
Your plans
  1. Tomorrow I’m going to work.
  2. Tomorrow I’m going to the masjid.
  3. Next week I’m going to visit my family.
Sample answers
  • بُكْرَة رَاح أَرُوح الشُّغُل.
  • بُكْرَة بَرُوح المَسْجِد.
  • الأُسْبُوع الجَاي رَاح أَزُور أَهْلِي.

3️⃣ Short Story – Yesterday / Last Weekend

This is from your “Stories & Past Experiences” idea (Lesson 21).

Write in Arabic (or translit first)

Tell a 3–4 line story:

  1. Yesterday I woke up at…
  2. I went to…
  3. I met…
  4. We ate / drank…
Sample mini-story
امس صَحِيت السَّاعَة ثَمَانِيَة.
بَعْدِين رُحْت الشُّغُل، وَرَجَعْت بَعْد العَصْر.
فِي اللِّيل رُحْت المَسْجِد، شُفْت أَصْحَابِي.
أَكَلْنَا سَوَى وَشْرِبْنَا شَاي.
ams ṣaḥīt as-sāʿah thamāniyah.
baʿdēn ruḥt ash-shughl, wa rajaʿt baʿd al-ʿaṣr.
fil-lēl ruḥt il-masjid, shift aṣḥābī.
akalnā sawā wa shribnā shāy.

4️⃣ Invitations, Saying Yes & No (Lesson 15)

Arabic Translit Meaning
تِجِي مَعَانَا؟ tijī maʿānā? Are you coming with us?
يَلَّا نِرُوح yallā nirūḥ Let’s go
مَا أَقْدَر اليَوْم mā agdar il-yōm I can’t today
مَرَّة نِثَانِيَة إن شاء الله marrah thāniyah in shā’ Allāh Another time in shā’ Allāh
Translate
  1. Bro, are you coming with us to the masjid?
  2. I can’t today, I’m busy.
  3. Another time in shā’ Allāh.
Sample answers
  • يا أَخُوي، تِجِي مَعَانَا المَسْجِد؟
  • مَا أَقْدَر اليَوْم، أَنَا مَشْغُول.
  • مَرَّة ثَانِيَة إن شاء الله.

5️⃣ WhatsApp Arabic (Lesson 16)

Super useful for Arab group chats with your mates.

Arabic (chat-style) Meaning
السلام عليكم يا شباب Salām guys
وينكم؟ Where are you lot?
جاهز؟ Ready?
وصلت I arrived
ثواني One sec
Write this as a WhatsApp message

“Salām guys, where are you? I’m waiting in the car.”

Sample
السلام عليكم يا شباب، وينكم؟ أنا أَسْتَنَّى في السيارة.

6️⃣ Restaurants & Complaints (Lessons 19 & 22)

Arabic Translit Meaning
الأَكْل بَارِد شْوَيّ il-akl bārid shwayy The food is a bit cold
مُمكِن تِسَخِّن لَنَا؟ mumkin tisakhkhin lanā? Can you heat it up for us?
الخِدْمَة بَطِيئَة شْوَيّ il-khidmah baṭī’ah shwayy The service is a bit slow
مَعْلِيش، بَسّ الطَّلَب تَأَخَّر maʿlēsh, bass aṭ-ṭalab ta’akhkhar Sorry, but the order is late
Translate
  1. The food is cold, can you heat it up, please?
  2. Sorry, but the tea is very late.
Sample answers
  • الأَكْل بَارِد، مُمكِن تِسَخِّن لَنَا لَوْ سَمَحْت؟
  • مَعْلِيش، بَسّ الشَّاي تَأَخَّر مَرَّة.

7️⃣ People & Personality (Lesson 20)

Arabic Translit Meaning
هُوَّ طَيِّب huwwa ṭayyib He is kind
هُوَّ جِدِّي شْوَيّ huwwa jiddī shwayy He is a bit serious
هِيَّ مَزْحُوقَة / مِزْحِية hiyya mazḥūgah / mizḥiyyah She’s jokey / likes to joke (dialectal)
هُوَّ هَادِي huwwa hādī He is calm
Describe 2 real people
  1. Describe one friend (name + 2 adjectives).
  2. Describe one family member (name + 2 adjectives).

8️⃣ Big Mixed Quiz – 11–20 in One Go

A. Translate into Arabic
  1. Yesterday I went with my friend to the restaurant.
  2. Tomorrow I’m going to visit my uncle.
  3. The food was a bit cold, but it was tasty.
  4. My friend is kind but serious.
  5. I can’t today, I’m busy, maybe another time in shā’ Allāh.
Sample answers
  • امس رُحْت مَع صَاحْبِي المَطْعَم.
  • بُكْرَة رَاح أَزُور عَمِّي.
  • الأَكْل كَان بَارِد شْوَيّ، بَسّ كَان لَذِيذ.
  • صَاحْبِي طَيِّب بَسّ جِدِّي شْوَيّ.
  • مَا أَقْدَر اليَوْم، أَنَا مَشْغُول، مُمْكِن مَرَّة ثَانِيَة إن شاء الله.
If you can do this lesson without panicking, you’ve basically got “real-life small talk + story mode” in Saudi / Gulf Arabic.
