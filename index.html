Saudi Arabic Course – Home

Your 6-month path to speaking real Saudi Arabic – Najdi core, Hijāzi flavour, Gulf twist.

How to Use This Course

This is your complete speaking-focused Arabic curriculum.

  1. Start with Lesson 1 and go in order.
  2. Do 2–3 lessons a week (60–90 minutes per lesson).
  3. Speak out loud — this course is built for conversation.
  4. Use Arabic in real life every week with Arabs in Birmingham.

Your Goals After 6 Months

  • Speak basic Saudi/Gulf Arabic naturally in real conversations.
  • Understand most replies from Saudis, Yemenis, Syrians, Jordanians, Palestinians.
  • Survive comfortably during ʿUmrah / Ḥajj (guards, shops, directions, complaints).
  • Join mixed Arab friend groups without feeling lost.
  • Catch the meaning of many Saudi/Gulf songs & vlogs.

You won’t be native, but you’ll be *legit*. Arabs will respect it.

Study Plan That Fits Your Life

Weekly Plan

  • 2 full lessons (Mon & Thu works well)
  • 10 minutes a day revising vocab
  • Use 1 phrase daily with a real Arab

Why This Works

You're a British Pakistani Muslim from Birmingham — you're around Arabs all the time. That real exposure will make your progress *twice* as fast.

FAQ – Is This Dialect Useful?

Will Arabs understand me?

Yes — especially Saudis, Yemenis, Syrians, Jordanians, Palestinians, Emirates, Qataris.

Is mixing Najdi + Hijāzi weird?

No. As a learner, it sounds completely normal — still clearly Saudi/Khaleeji.

Will this help with ʿUmrah?

Massively. You’ll handle guards, shops, directions, basic arguments, everything.
