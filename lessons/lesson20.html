Lesson 20 – People & Personality

Describe your friends, family and random Saudis you meet. Say who is funny, kind, annoying, serious, generous, shy, etc.

Level: A2 → B1 Focus: Describing people Time: ~90 minutes

1️⃣ Adjectives for Personality

ArabicTranslitMeaning
طَيِّبṭayyibkind / good-hearted
هَادِيhādīcalm
مِزْعِجmizʿijannoying
مَرِحmariḥfunny / cheerful
جَادّjāddserious
كَرِيمkarīmgenerous
بَخِيلbakhīlstingy
مُهَذَّبmuhadhdhabpolite
مِنْتَظِمmuntazimorganized

Feminine forms add -ah
طَيِّب → طَيِّبَة • كَرِيم → كَرِيمَة

2️⃣ Useful Sentence Patterns

هُوّ طَيِّب • huwwa ṭayyib – He is kind
هِيّ كَرِيمَة • hiyya karīmah – She is generous
أَخُوي مَرِح • akhūy mariḥ – My brother is funny
Translate
  1. My friend is serious.
  2. He is annoying.
  3. She is calm.
  • صاحِبِي جَادّ
  • هُوّ مِزْعِج
  • هِيّ هَادِيَة

3️⃣ Talking About People You Know

صَاحِبِي سَعُودِي وَمَرِح مَرَّة
ṣāḥibī saʿūdī wa mariḥ marrah

“marrah” = very (Saudi/hijazi)

Fill In
  1. _______ بَاكِسْتَانِي وَ_______ كَرِيم.
  2. صَاحِبْتِي _______ وَ_______.

4️⃣ Dialogue – Describing Friends

أ: كَيْف صَاحِبَك؟
ب: طَيِّب وَكَرِيم، بَسّ شْوَيّ مِزْعِج.
أ: هَهَهَه، عَاد هَذَا أَكْثَر الشَّبَاب!
A: How’s your friend?
B: Kind and generous, but a bit annoying.
A: Haha, that’s most guys!

5️⃣ Compliments & Insults (Polite)

  • مُؤَدَّب – mū’addab – respectful
  • قَلِيل أَدَب – qalīl adab – rude (careful!)
  • عَقِل – ʿagil – mature / sensible
  • مِسْكِين – miskīn – poor guy (sympathy)

6️⃣ Speaking Drills

Say these 5 times each:

هُوّ كَرِيم مَرّة
أَخُوي مِزْعِج شْوَيّ
هِيّ مُؤَدَّبَة

7️⃣ End-of-Lesson Quiz

  1. Translate: “My sister is kind.”
  2. Translate: “He is very funny.”
  3. Translate: “Your friend is rude.” (m)
  • أُخْتِي طَيِّبَة
  • هُوّ مَرِح مَرّة
  • صَاحِبَك قَلِيل أَدَب
